At InPhinite Design our philosophy is simple, great customer interaction can make or break a company - our services provide you with the ability to make it.


A web and mobile app development firm, InPhinite Design was created in reaction to the generic and overly simplified current standard of web design. To combat this limited style we we work directly with our clients to construct a unique web experience that will convey their brand across as many platforms as desired.

InPhinite Design was founded in 2014 by Collin Brown, Mitchell Popen, and Tanishq Suryavanshi. Together we work with a group of highly skilled artists, designers, developers, and writers to create specific and customized content to satisfy the widest range of clientele needs.

We provide a full service approach to web and company development, offering our clients everything from logo and website design, to advertisement, branding, and code debugging.
